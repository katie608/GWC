Sleep Deprivation in Teenagers

Sleep is very important and lack of sleep can have numerous detrimental effects on the mind and body. Concequences

There are many concequences to sleep deprivation, especially in teenagers. One of the most important is decreased mental ability. This is bad because it leads teenagers to preform lower in school. Other concequences include increased chance of mental illnesses such as depression, and suicide attempts. Sleep deprivation can also lead to an unhealthy weight and eating habits as the lack of sleep inhibits the parts of the brain that control hunger.
